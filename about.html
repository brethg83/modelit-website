About Model IT

Our Mission

Model IT was created with a simple belief: everyone has unique style worth sharing, and that style has value. We're building a platform where everyday fashion enthusiasts can turn their personal style into a source of income.

The Problem We're Solving

Traditional fashion influence has been gatekept by professional models, celebrities, and major influencers. Meanwhile, everyday people with incredible style have no way to monetize their fashion sense. Online shopping lacks the personal touch of seeing how clothes look on real people in real situations.

Our Solution

Model IT bridges the gap between personal style and social commerce. Our platform allows anyone to:

  • Share photos of their outfits with product tags
  • Build a following based on their unique aesthetic
  • Earn commissions when others purchase items they recommend
  • Discover authentic fashion inspiration from people who look like them

How We Make Money

Model IT partners with major retailers and affiliate networks to provide shoppable links for fashion products. When users make purchases through our platform, we earn a commission which we split with our content creators. This creates a sustainable ecosystem where everyone benefits.

Our Commitment

We're committed to:

  • Fair revenue sharing with our creator community
  • Authentic, diverse fashion representation
  • User privacy and data protection
  • Building tools that empower individual style expression
  • Sustainable and ethical fashion practices

Join Our Community

Whether you're a fashion enthusiast looking to share your style, or a shopper seeking authentic outfit inspiration, Model IT is your platform. Download our app today and start your journey as a fashion influencer.
